Who We Are

All Clear Collective (ACC) is a group of dancers, teachers, and makers based in the Netherlands. We create performances, workshops, and artistic encounters that explore movement as connection — between people, between ideas, between worlds.

Our Work

We move between teaching and performing, from intimate studio practice to large-scale productions. Each project begins with curiosity — what happens when we meet, move, and create together? Through dance, we build spaces of honesty, rhythm, and imagination.

Our Approach

Collaboration is at the heart of everything we do. We share authorship, support each other’s voices, and believe in creative responsibility. Every dancer brings a different story — together, we form a collective body that keeps moving.

“We dance to stay awake — together.”
