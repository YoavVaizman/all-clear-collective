A physical study on strength and vulnerability. Soft Armor unfolds as a series of movements that question what protection means — when the body becomes both shield and language. The work explores resilience through sensitivity, inviting the audience to witness honesty in motion.

A live performance that listens to the body as an instrument of persistence. Dancers pulse, breathe, and repeat — finding rhythm in exhaustion. Hope Frequency is a meditation on the practice of staying in motion when everything wants to stop.

Grounded Light

A site-specific performance that transforms urban and natural spaces into temporary stages. Grounded Light explores how we move through our surroundings — and how our surroundings move us. The piece merges choreography, sound, and architecture into one breathing organism.
